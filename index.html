Our Story

Founded in 2010, Cake Delight began as a small home bakery with a passion for creating delicious, beautiful cakes. What started as a hobby quickly grew into a beloved local bakery known for our attention to detail and commitment to quality.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of happy customers with our wide range of cakes, cupcakes, and desserts. Each creation is made with the finest ingredients and a whole lot of love.
